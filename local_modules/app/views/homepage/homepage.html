Built for humans.

Arrays is designed with the everyday user in mind. Upload projects and start exploring your data immediately.

Arrays will support the following scenarios:

  • Create an interactive product inventory for your business.
  • Give your staff a single place to go for easy access to mission-critical documents.
  • Merge a list of conference attendees with information from LinkedIn to create a custom CRM database.
  • Chart your personal health data from your doc and self-tracking to get a better picture of your health.
  • Track campaign financing among all the candidates for the 2016 presidential and local elections.
  • Combine the digital collections data from several museums to create a more complete picture of art historical movements.
  • …And many more!
