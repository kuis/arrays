Quick Facts

  • Atlas is an internal Amgen web application designed to provide scientific staff and management with efficient access to important molecule information.
  • Atlas was developed as an innovative replacement for Molecule Summary Reports, which had been created and managed by the Analytical Sciences group in Process & Product Development (P&PD); its development occurred as part of the P&PD Design Thinking program.
  • Version 1.0 (published: 2015-03-01) contains a collection of important molecule information for more than 40 molecules that have gone through Process Development at Amgen over the past decade.

A Very Brief Project History

In 2013, a few P&PD staff learned that a few P&PD leaders were disturbed by the fact that Molecule Summary Reports - important sources of molecule information - were not being created and maintained properly. A project team self-organized and got the support of P&PD Management to use Design Thinking to address the Molecule Summary Report (MSR) issue.

In early 2014, with an eye toward the P&PD Innovation Summit, and inspired by the Apple Knowledge Navigator video, the MSR project team partnered with Christian Marc Schmidt of Schema Design to develop a vision of what Molecule Summary Reports (or their replacement) might look like in a decade or two. Christian (IDEO, Microsoft) has expertise in interface design and data visualization. Combining that with our team's scientific expertise, we created a prototype web application and shared it via Surface Pro tablets at the Summit.

Following an enthusiastic response to our vision work, we secured funding from P&PD Management to create version 1 of the application we envisioned — Atlas.
